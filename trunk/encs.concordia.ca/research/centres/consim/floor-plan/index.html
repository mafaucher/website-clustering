Floor Plan

 



EV16.220 (Gowning room)
The gowning area supplies clean room garments for the ConSiM and ECE users.

EV16.219 (Thin Film)
Thin film process is the primary function of ConSiM Lab. As a class 1,000 facility it contains the processing equipment for PECVD, DRIE (IPC 65) and the Ar E-Bean.

EV16.218 (Lithography)
The DWL 66 laser lithography system is a high resolution pattern generator for mask making and direct wafer writing. The capabilities and flexibility of this system make it the ultimate lithographic research tool in MEMS and all other applications that require microstructures.

EV 16.217 (Bench)
The yellow room has wet bench, laminar flow bench, a spin coater, an, oven and a DI water system.

EV16.215, 225 & 236 (ECE Lab)
The exploratory area of ECE is a class 10,000 area used for exploratory materials and processes involving equipment which is highly specialized for ECE research.

EV 16.205 (Gas storage room)
This room contains the bulk of the liquid N2 as well as the N2O and other special gasses.
