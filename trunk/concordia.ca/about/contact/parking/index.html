      • Operating hours and costs:
        • Monday to Friday, 6 a.m. - midnight
          • $3.25 half an hour
          • $5.25 one hour
          • $7.50 one hour & a half
          • $10.50 Exit before 6 p.m.
          • $6.25 Entry after 5 p.m.
          • $12.75 Daily maximum
          • $20 for a lost ticket
        • Saturday (September to May) 7 a.m. to 8 p.m., and Sunday, 9 a.m. - 8 p.m.
          • $6.25 flat rate
      • Faubourg garage entrance (1250 Guy, View the map)
        • Operating hours and costs:
          • Monday to Friday, 6 a.m. - midnight

            • $6.25 Entry after 4 p.m.
            • $12.75 Daily maximum
            • $20 for a lost ticket
          • Saturday (September to May) 7 a.m. to 8 p.m., and Sunday, 9 a.m. - 8 p.m.
            • $6.25 flat rate

      • Cash
      • MasterCard
      • Visa
