Campus bus stops

  • Hall Building out front, 1455 De Maisonneuve West, SGW Campus
  • Loyola Chapel 7137 Sherbrooke St West, Loyola Campus

Please note…

  • The ride between the SGW and Loyola campuses takes approximately 20 minutes. It beats public transportation
  • It's free but a Concordia student, faculty, staff or alumni ID card is required
  • Buses are wheelchair accessible except for replacements
  • Departure times are approximate and may vary due to unexpected circumstances, traffic and/or weather
