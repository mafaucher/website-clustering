Graduate Program Directors List

Each program in the University is administered primarily by a Graduate Program Director (GPD). The GPD is responsible for advising graduate students on any academic matters concerning their program. Visit our GPD List section to find a list of all program directors in each faculty.

Graduate Program Assistants

Each program also has a Graduate Program Assistant. The Assistants are responsible for helping students with administrative issues (i.e. course registration, course withdrawal, student requests) and, in some departments, will assist the GPD in advising students on academic matters. Although assistants may advise students on administrative matters, it is the student’s responsibility for knowing the rules, regulations, policies and deadlines in the Graduate Calendar. See a list of Graduate Program Assistants.

Guidelines for Concordia Supervisors and Graduate Students
