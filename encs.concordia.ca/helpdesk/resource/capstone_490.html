Capstone 490 Design Projects

This web page provides students with information and plotter support for the CAPSTONE design projects BLDG 490, CIVI 490, COMP 490, SOEN 490, COEN 490, ELEC 490, INDU 490, and MECH 490. For the course listing and the computer lab location go here. For a general overview, you may access the Faculty of Engineering Capstone Projects Centre.

    • General Plotter Information

      • This information is specifically related to the CAPSTONE presentations. Please note that during this time, three plotters are available to students. During the normal course of the year, only RASP and CHISEL are accessible.

    • Plotting Procedures & Requirements

      • Availability: The plotters are available to students by appointment only. During the CAPSTONE presentation period, add your Group information to the reservation forms posted inside H961-14. Outside this period, send a request to helpdesk@encs.concordia.ca.

      • Procedure: When sending a job to the plotter, we suggest you send the file in PDF format. Here is a simple example on how to save a file in PDF format and send it to CHISEL. The following is the link to the AutoCAD FAQ and the Graphics Lab FAQs . It is highly recommended to convert your AutoCAD drawing to Adobe PDF files prior to plotting.         NOTE: If you require extra quota for the plotter, we will require an email from your supervisor.
