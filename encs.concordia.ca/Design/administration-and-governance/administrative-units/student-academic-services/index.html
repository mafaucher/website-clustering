Information for International Students

The International Students Office (ISO) offers information regarding finances, housing, tuition, health insurance and more, for all new international students. To download the ISO's Information Guide or sign-up to the ISO E-mail Information Bulletin, consult the ISO website.



Our office is an integral part of the Dean's Office and is mandated, along with the individual academic departments in the Faculty of Engineering and Computer Science (ENCS), to look after the academic and administrative needs of undergraduate and graduate students, both current and new. We are also foremost responsible for admitting all students to ENCS, and actively assist potential students in discovering the programs and entrance requirements for our Faculty. To find out more about these and other services we offer, we strongly encourage you to look over our website.
