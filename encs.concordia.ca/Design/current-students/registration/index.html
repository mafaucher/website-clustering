Student Academic Services Office

Student Academic Services (SAS) is the office that deals with admissions and advising. SAS is the place to go to: obtain general advising; receive general information about ENCS Departments; inquire about the admission requirements for programs offered by ENCS; find out what is needed to change programs; access information on transfer credits, student exchanges, visiting students and student requests; and apply for readmission.

If you need additional information please do not hesitate to contact SAS by phoning +1-514-848-2424, ext. 3055 or 3057.

Quebec Inter-university Exchange Program

An agreement between Quebec universities allows students registered as regular students in one Québec university (the home university) to follow, within the framework of their program of study, one or more courses of their choice, for valid reasons, at another Québec university. Find out more from the CREPUQ website.

Course Sequences

Undergraduate students accepted to Engineering programs, who have completed all required prerequisites, follow a sequence that would lead to graduation after four years (8 terms) of study. ENCS students must follow a set sequence of their programs as listed in the Course Sequences section of this website.
