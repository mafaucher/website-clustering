How to access an AITS managed computer from home
Faculty and Staff members can access their office computer from home.

Requirements:

 - Your office computer needs to be an AITS managed computer built after September 2006
- You must have logged on at least once on the computer you are trying to access (you need to have a local account)
- You need to download and install UltraVNC on your home computer
- You need to copy the encryption key  from your Office computer to your home computer
- You need to establish an ssh tunnel to the AITS network. We recommend putty.exe for this.

Steps:

Install UltraVNC on your home computer (needs to be done only once)

1. Download and install UltraVNC on your home computer

2.  Download the MSRC4 plugin and copy it into the UltraVNC folder (by default UltraVNC is installed in C:\Program Files\UltraVNC)

3. Copy the file rc4.key into the UltraVNC folder. You can copy this file from your Office PC, the file is located in C:\Program Files\UltraVNC

 
Install and configure putty.exe on your home computer (needs to be done only once)

4. Download putty.exe

5. Launch putty.exe and set up ssh tunnel. In the left side window, expand "SSH" by clicking on the "+" sign and then select "Tunnels"



6. Configure the tunnel:
        - In the source port box, enter a number between 49152 and 65535
        - In the destination box, enter the full name of your Office computer (or it's IP address) followed by :5900



        - Then click on "Add"




7. In the left side window, scroll to the top and click on "Session"



8. In the "Host Name" box enter tunnel.encs.concordia.ca
         - Ensure that "Port" is set to 22 and SSH is selected
   
    In the "Saved Sessions" window, enter a name (e.g., VNC_tunnel) and then click on save so that the above putty steps will be saved.




For all future connections, you can "Load" the saved session

Connecting to your Office Computer

After you have installed and configured UltraVNC and Putty, connecting to your Office computer is a two step procedure. First, you will need to establish the tunnel, then by using UltraVNC to connect to your Office Computer.

A. Launch putty.exe , select your saved session (VNC_tunnel) and click load




B. Click on "open". Your first connection will prompt you with a "Putty Security Alert", simply click "Yes" to accept. You will then need to enter your ENCS username and password in the terminal window. This window needs to stay open as long as you are connected to your Office Computer. Do not close this window until after you have logged off your Office computer.



C.  Once you have sucessfully logged on, you can use UltraVNC to connect to your office computer. Launch UltraVNC.

 

        - In the VNC Server box, enter localhost:50001
            The number must be same number you used as the "source Port" in step 6.

       - Select "Use DSM Plugin" and ensure that MSRC4Plugin_NoReg.dsm is in the drop down window.




        - Click connect. There will be a 20 second delay, after which you will need to enter your ENCS username and password twice.
