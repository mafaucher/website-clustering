ENCS Pay-to-Print

ENCS Managed Desktop Solution

ENCS provides "pay-to-print" printers which students can use to print when they run out of quota. Currently the following printers configured this way:
PRINTER ROOM
NIB H0966-01
CHALK H0806-03


To use this service, first send your print job to a pay-to-print printer, then go to the printer room. You must now use a "release" station to pay for your print job(s). The "release" station is a PC in the printer room with the following display.




Insert your debit card into the card reader, you will then be prompted for a "User Id".




Type your username in the text box and press "OK". You will then be shown a list of your current print jobs. Print jobs are automatically deleted after 120 minutes.




Select the print jobs you want to print, you will be shown the number of pages, the cost and your card balance. If this is satisfactory, click on "Print Selected Jobs" and your print job will be queued to print. Again, unselected print jobs will be deleted after 120 minutes. Please remember to take your debit card.
