Campus and Group Tours

Campus Tours

Learn more about Concordia, our student life, and Montreal in general! Campus tours, given by current Concordia students, are offered year-round to interested students, parents and friends. Guests also have the opportunity to request an individual advising session with a general interviewer to answer any specific questions they may have. (Please note that you may also be touring with other prospective students).

Schedule
Campus tours take place Monday to Friday, and can include both campuses should a guest be interested.

Booking a Campus Tour
Tours may be reserved through Connect2.Concordia.ca. We encourage you to specify your programs of interest so that specific information can be included in the tour. Please note that individual advising sessions with an interviewer are limited and require advanced notice.

If you need assistance with your tour reservation, please contact our Welcome Centre Coordinator at 514-848-2424 ext. 4779 or by email, tours@concordia.ca.

Group Tours

Group tours are tailored to the needs and wishes of visitors, and our team is delighted to provide access to departmental representatives or advisors. In addition, our Recruitment Officers provide groups with formal presentations, highlighting the university and answering any particular admissions questions that arise.

Booking a Group Tour
For visiting school groups (larger than 10 people) please email tours@concordia.ca or call the Welcome Centre Coordinator at 514-848-2424 ext. 4779 to organize a tour.

Departmental tours for graduate students

For a guided tour of research labs and facilities or to book an appointment with a graduate program director of a specific program, graduate students should contact gradtour@alcor.concordia.ca.
